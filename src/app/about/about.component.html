About Us

Cameron DeMille

Founder - Front End Developer

Cameron is a marketing and website consultant. He started robotics on a rookie FTC team in 2013 during his freshman year of high school. He competed in 2 years of FTC and 1 year of FRC. This will be his 3rd year as a FTC mentor and 5th year as a FLL mentor.

He feels that the FIRST community had a large impact on his life and loves to give back to the community. When he is not volunteering at FLL/FTC competitions, he spends most of his time mentoring/coaching FTC and FLL teams. His goal is to get as many people involved in robotics as he can.

Web developing is also another passion of his, as he enjoys researching new technologies and ways to develop websites. He also enjoys cycling and running.
